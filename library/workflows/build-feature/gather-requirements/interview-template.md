# Interview Template

## Problem Space
- What problem are you trying to solve?
- Who is affected by this problem?
- How are they currently working around it?
- What is the impact of not solving it?

## Success Criteria
- What does "done" look like?
- How will you measure success?
- What is the minimum viable version?

## Constraints
- Are there technology or platform constraints?
- What is the timeline?
- Are there budget or resource limitations?
- Any security or compliance requirements?

## Integration
- What existing systems does this touch?
- Are there APIs or data sources involved?
- Who else needs to be consulted?
